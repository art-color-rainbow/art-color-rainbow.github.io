About Me

I am Vishakha Isapure, working as a contemporary artist. I completed my post-graduation in Fine Arts from Kavikulaguru Kalidas Sanskrit University. My work is dedicated to translating unspoken emotions and stories into visual form, blending classical techniques with deep emotional expression.

I see art as a language of its ownâ€”a way to shape thoughts and feelings that words often fail to capture. My creative practice explores themes of inner conflict, silent struggles, and the delicate balance between humanity and nature.

I am especially inspired by the emotional landscapes we carry within, as well as the visible and invisible marks we leave on the natural world. Through layered textures, thoughtful forms, and subtle color palettes, my paintings create quiet spaces for reflection and connection.

About My Artwork

Studying Fine Arts shaped both my technical skills and artistic vision, giving me a strong foundation in still life, portraiture, and conceptual painting. Early on, I focused on realistic works exploring feminine emotion and human expression. This taught me the power of observation, detail, and emotional depth.

Over time, my practice shifted toward deeper creative exploration, moving beyond realism to develop a personal style centered on silent emotion and the inner battles often left unspoken. Currently, my work focuses on mental breakdown, emotional struggle, and environmental decay. Using layered textures, organic forms, and symbolic imagery, I often blend subconscious emotions with forms and patterns I observe in nature, reflecting the deep connection between inner feelings and the natural world.
